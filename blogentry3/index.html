BLOG ENTRY #3


At this point I started creating the website blog, documenting a mood that the brand embodies as a whole. That theme is dark and ominous, but also combined with a more modern and minimal approach. Most of the images on the blog encapture a sense of the brand name; white noise. Thus, images with confusing and blurry effects, mostly dark and monochromatic but also some with tasteful color added, were included in the blog.

The screenshots below show the process of making the blog. To make the blog more interactive, I will make sure to add microinteractins within the blog, and add gifs with constantly changing content to add a sense of movement to the page.





The major pages that need to be developed include the navigation page, and the product pages themselves. Considering that there is a large amount of products on the website, and they are all so similar, I was thinking that maybe having a page for each is not the best option for designing the website, but also for the user too, as he would have to click on a variety of pages just go to through basic product information.

Therefore, I started building a sample product page, shown in the screenshot below:





Since having a page per product could potentially be a little overwhelming for the user to keep clicking and keep getting re-routed into another page, I started considering how the sample product page above would look and work if it was in the form of a pop-up style page, when you click over the product, to create a small box over the page were you can add the product to your cart.
